After graduating from Middlesex University in the UK, b.wing returned to Hong Kong and discovered a love of, and a special talent for, creating installation art and illustrations. Daring to embrace change and challenge convention, she began her career as a full-time artist in 2002.

b.wing’s talent was soon recognised by the Asian art circle. In 2003, she held her first solo exhibition if you don’t want to be perfect, you’ve come to the right place at agnès b. Librairie Galerie in Hong Kong.The same exhibition was then staged at agnès b. gallery in Taipei, Taiwan as well as agnès b. Aoyama store in Tokyo, Japan.
