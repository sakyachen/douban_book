    咪咪嚕外灘迷失記
    The Adventure of Mimilu,the Wandering Cat
    鮮花在茶几上散髮著香氣，咪咪嚕覺得自己是客廳的小主人。
    The flowers spread trails of scent in the air.
    Meowing and sniffing around the coffee table,
    Mimilu felt like the queen of the house.
    每天晚上的鋼琴課，也是咪咪嚕的音樂會。
    Every night ，Mimilu enjoyed music
    while students were having piano lessons.
    咪咪嚕沒有出過門，它喜歡看著樹影發呆。
    Mimilu had never stepped out of the house before.
    She loved pondering the mysteries of the colorful trees.
    有一天，它溜出門，沿著復興西路走啊……
    One day ,Mimilu sneaked out of her house.
    She wandered along West Fuxing Road for a long way...
    就這樣來到了外灘。
    and she finally arrived at the Bund.
    "哦，原來它不會動。"
    “Oh,the lion is motionless.”
    "在高處，可以看得更遠。"
    "King of the hill,top of the heap……"
    地毯好軟，從大門口一直鋪到頂樓。
    Soft red carpets covered the stairs all the way from the front door to the roof.
    樓頂很熱鬧，但誰也沒留意這隻小貓。
    The roof was full people,but nobady paid attention to this little cat.
    "他們在幹什麼？"
    “What are they doing?”
    "呀，一隻貓咪！"
    “Hey，where did you come from?”
    "我們一起拍照好嗎？"
    "Shall we take pictures together?"
    拍了好多照片。瞧，她多可愛！
    The bride took a lot of picture with Mimilu.
    She looked so lovely!
    "謝謝你。我回家嘍~！"
    “Thank you，kitty!We are going home now.”
    "啦啦啦，啦啦啦……"
    “Lalala,lalala……”
    “爸爸快看，橋上有隻貓！”
    “Dad，look!There is a cat on the bridge!”
    “橋上哪兒來的貓啊，走吧……”
    “There isn't any cat. Let's go……”
    “沒人跟我玩，我就跟口香糖玩。”
    “Noboby wants to play with me.I can have fun with a piece of chewing gum.”
    有點累了……
    Mimilu was tired……
    哪兒有好吃的東西？
    Where could she find yummy food?
    天也黑了。
    It was also getting dark.
    "你是新來的吧？外灘所有的流浪貓我都認識，沒見過你呀！"
    “Are you a newcome?I know every stray cat in the Bund,but I have never seen you before!”
    "吃吧，孩子，吃吧。"
    “Come here，kitty.This is for you.”
    "我要去巡夜了，你跟我來嗎？"
    “I am going to patrol around.Would you like to come with me?”
    "這是銀行金庫，我看了四十年了。"
    “This is the bank vault.I have been guarind it foe 40 years.”
    "今天，我就退休了。"
    “Today,I am retiring.”
    "太陽升起來了，不能陪你了，小傢伙。"
    “The sun is rising. I have to leave you alone,my little one.”
    "我給你帶來個新朋友。"
    “I have brought you a new friend.”
    "我要走了，握握手吧。"
    “I am leaving. Take care,kitty.”
    "小傢伙，不許搗亂。"
    “Hi,little one!Please do not mess around.”
    "哦，亮了。"
    “Oh,it is lit up.”
    "我是管燈的。燈不亮的時候，就要去檢查。"
    “I am a lighting technician.Where a building is not lit up,I need to climb up and check the wires.”
    "把你的照片傳上網，也許可以幫你找到家。"
    “I am going to upload your picture to the Internet.”
    "今天晚上你負責開燈吧。按那個最大的按鈕。"
    “Tonight,I am going to let you light up the Bound.
    Here,press the largest button.”
    10,9,8,7,6……
    “哇，這麼亮!”
    "Wow,it is so bright!"
    從那以後，每個晚上，他們都在一起給外灘開燈。
    After that, they lit up the Bund together every night.
    又過了一些日子……
    Time had passed by……
    “家找到了！”
    “I have foundyour home!”
    咪咪嚕回到了復興西路家中，人們已經忘記了它的迷失故事。
    Mimilu finally returned to her home at West Fuing Road.
    咪咪嚕也和往常一樣，只是它經常會爬上屋頂朝外灘方向張望，什麼時候再幫叔叔把外灘的燈打亮。
    （完）
    蘑菇豆丁錄入，請勿轉載。
